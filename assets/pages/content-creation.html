TASKS AND EVALUATION

We've designed a dual-point system to reward and motivate our ambassadors. Both point types work together to recognize your contributions and help you climb the ranks in the Solus community.

Engagement Points

A key metric used to increase and maintain your rank within the program.

Use the /set twitter command with your Twitter username to link your Twitter account to your discord account in #engage-leaderbord channel, for more info check #solus-quest pinned message.

Ambassador Points

Another essential currency for rank progression and maintenance, earned by completing a variety of tasks available on the taskboard.

After completing the task, proceed to the channel - #content-submit
Type /myprofile in the text box.
To submit work for review, click Send Work, select the Category and Specific Task you have completed, and then send the link to your work in the chat.
You will receive a notification in the #amb-review channel once your work is reviewed.
